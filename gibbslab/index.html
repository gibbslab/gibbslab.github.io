GiBBS Laboratory

GiBBS computational laboratory is located at Institute for Genetics, National University of Colombia.

Our lab consists of various desktop machines, an intermediate capacity server (100GB RAM, 27 CPU cores) and a hyper convergent DELL VRTX cluster solution with the following overall characteristics:

  • Four power Edge M640 blade servers.
  • RAM: 2.8 TB
  • Storage: 120 TB
  • 280 CPU cores Xeon Gold 2.6 GhZ
