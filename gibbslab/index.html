GiBBS Computational Laboratory

GiBBS computational laboratory is located at Institute for Genetics (IGUN), National University of Colombia (UN).

Our lab consists of various desktop machines, an intermediate capacity server (100GB RAM, 27 CPU cores) and a hyper convergent DELL VRTX cluster solution with the following overall characteristics:

  • Four power Edge M640 blade servers.
  • RAM: 2.8 TB
  • Storage: 120 TB
  • 280 CPU cores Xeon Gold 2.6 GhZ

Most Admin tasks are supported by engineer Javier Duque.

Access to Cluster Servers

All students and researchers associated to the Institute for Genetics (IGUN) have granted access to our computational platform, external users will be evaluated in a case by case basis.

Nevertheles both users have to fullfill a general form and agree to our ACCESS POLICY before accesing the platform.
